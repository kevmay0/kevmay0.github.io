Conceptual Design Overview

This vehicle is designed to load, transport, and deploy large and heavy cargo on the lunar surface. It utilizes NASA's Lightweight Surface Manipulation System and a scissor-hinge mechanism to load cargo of various sizes.

VEHICLE MASS 70,000 kg
